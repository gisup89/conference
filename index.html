Welcome -

I am pleased to announce that Korean Society of Transportation hosts the 1st International Conference in Seoul, Korea on 29th-30th October 2022. This event comes along with the 40th anniversary of the Korean Society of Transportation.

The Korean Society of Transportation was established on November 18, 1982, for the purpose of ultimately contributing to national development by promoting academic, practical, and policy development and mutual exchange among members. After that, it was registered as a corporation on April 15, 1989, and currently 4,599 individual members with 141 cooperate (special) members.

For academic activities, we regularly publish the Journal of the Korean Transportation Association 6 times a year to help facilitate academic and technical exchanges among members. The Korean Transportation Society Academic Award is presented every year by carefully selecting creative and original research papers from among the research papers published in the journal. In addition, transportation technology and policies are published 6 times a year and are used as a forum for information exchange to inform the activities of the society and its members through policy reviews, discussion boards, discussion forums, key notes, side road stories, reader submissions, news and information.

An academic conference is held twice a year to present more than 290 papers a year, and academic debates are held more than four times a year to analyze transportation policy and suggest alternatives. Meanwhile, for academic specialization, 16 research groups and 15 specialized committees are formed in the academic committee, and each member registers in a field of interest to maximize the effect of research activities in the field of study and develop research standards and various analytical techniques through subcommittee meetings.

Our 1st international conference 2022 covers all aspects related to transportation and mobility and welcomes both scientific papers and presentations of business cases.

We are also pleased to announce that a collection of top scientific papers will be reviewed for a special issue of the Journal of Advanced Transportation.

Looking forward to seeing you in Seoul in October!



History

The Korean Society of Transportation(KST) was established on November 18, 1982 for the purpose of ultimately contributing to national development by promoting academic, practical, and policy development. In addition, KST holds academic presentations twice a year to publish more than 290 papers a year, and holds academic debates more than four times a year to analyze transportation policy and present alternatives. In commemoration of the 40th anniversary of KST establishment, we would like to start international research cooperation by promoting it as an international event.
